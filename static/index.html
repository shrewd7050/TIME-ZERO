⚔️ Battle Manual

Moves:

  • 💥 Heavy Attack – deals 20 damage,when other user is defending. If not attacker takes 15 damage
  • ⚡ Quick Attack – deals 15 damage,when other user uses heavy attack. If not attacker takes 10 damage
  • 🛡️ Defend – Blocking doesn't work against heavy attack ,denfender takes 20 damage. Else deals 10 damage to attacker
  • 🚫 Same Attack – Heavy attack and Denfence cancel out each other while Quick attacker take 5 damage each,

How it works:

  • • In Single Player, you and the CPM choose a move each round.
  • • The CPM “thinks” for a second before revealing its move.
  • • In Online Two Player, players take turns – the server validates each move.
  • • Winner gets +1 win and +1 level (saved to database).
